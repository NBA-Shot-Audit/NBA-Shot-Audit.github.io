Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

What We Do

NBAShotAudit is an automated service that generates and publishes NBA player highlight videos to social media platforms including X (Twitter) and TikTok.

Information We Collect

NBAShotAudit does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. We do not have user accounts, logins, or any form of user registration.

The only data we access is publicly available NBA game data (box scores, play-by-play, and highlight videos) provided through official NBA and related sports data sources.

TikTok API Data

We use the TikTok API solely to publish video content to our own TikTok account. We do not access, collect, or store any TikTok user data. We do not use TikTok data for any purpose other than publishing our own content.

Third-Party Services

We interact with the following third-party APIs solely for content publication:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to publish highlight videos
  • X (Twitter) API — to publish highlight videos and tweets
  • NBA.com — to access publicly available game data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at nbashotaudit@gmail.com.
