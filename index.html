About

What NBAShotAudit is and why it exists.

🏀

What We Do

Every night during the NBA season, we automatically generate highlight reels for the league's top performers — capturing every shot attempt and compiling them into player-specific videos.

🔎

Why It Exists

Traditional highlight reels cherry-pick the best moments. NBAShotAudit shows every shot — makes and misses — giving fans an unfiltered look at how players actually performed.

⚙️

The Technology

Our pipeline automatically discovers games, identifies standout performances, captures video from official NBA sources, edits compilations, and publishes across multiple platforms — all without manual intervention.

How It Works

From final buzzer to your feed — fully automated.

Game Discovery

After games end, we scan the NBA schedule and identify completed matchups with finalized box scores.

Player Selection

Star players are always tracked. Other players are auto-included when they hit standout scoring thresholds (30+ points).

Video Capture

Every field goal attempt for each selected player is captured from official NBA highlight sources and compiled into a single video.

Publish

Videos are posted to X, YouTube, and TikTok with game stats and context — ready for fans to watch by morning.
